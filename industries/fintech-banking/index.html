AI Models That Understand UAE Financial Services

From fraud detection to credit scoring, we build and operate ML models tuned to UAE card schemes, CBUAE regulations, and GCC transaction patterns.

UAE financial services is one of the highest-value verticals for custom AI models - high transaction volumes, regulatory compliance requirements that create AI use cases, and competitive pressure from regional neobanks that are AI-native from day one.

Fraud Detection: The Core Fintech AI Use Case

UAE payment fraud has distinctive characteristics that make generic Western fraud models underperform: high cross-border transaction volume (UAE is a major regional hub), specific GCC merchant category fraud patterns, and fraud velocity that exploits UAE’s expatriate population turnover. A fraud model must be trained on UAE transaction history to recognise these patterns.

Our fraud detection models are trained on your card scheme and transaction data with UAE-specific features: merchant country distribution, expat lifecycle signals, remittance corridor patterns, and device fingerprint analytics. We benchmark against your existing rule-based system before deployment - every model must demonstrably reduce fraud losses or false positives before going live.

Credit Scoring for UAE’s Thin-File Population

UAE’s credit scoring challenge is unique: 88% of residents are expatriates, many of whom have no UAE Al Etihad Credit Bureau history despite being creditworthy. Traditional scoring models exclude this population or assign poor scores based on absence of data.

Our alternative credit scoring models incorporate permissioned data sources that signal creditworthiness without bureau dependency: regular salary transfers, utility payment history, telecom bill payment records, and digital wallet transaction patterns. These models serve the underbanked expatriate segment while meeting CBUAE responsible lending requirements.

CBUAE Regulatory AI Compliance

The CBUAE’s 2023 AI Principles apply to all AI systems used in UAE-licensed financial institutions. Our fintech AI builds are designed for compliance from the start: explainable predictions, bias testing across UAE demographic segments, robustness testing against adversarial inputs, and full model documentation for CBUAE supervisory review.
