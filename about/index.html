About mlai.ae

mlai.ae is the UAE’s specialist Vertical AI consultancy - part of the NomadX family of technology practices based in Dubai.

We build and operate custom machine learning models for four UAE growth industries: Fintech & Banking, Medtech & Healthcare, Proptech & Real Estate, and Retail & eCommerce. Our focus is domain-specific AI - models trained on your industry data that outperform generic foundation models on your specific business problems.

What We Do

We deliver two things: Build and Operate.

Build means developing custom AI models from your domain data - data pipeline engineering, feature engineering, model training, evaluation, and production deployment with a complete MLOps pipeline. We also fine-tune foundation models (LLMs, vision models) on proprietary domain corpora, and integrate AI models into your existing business systems.

Operate means keeping your models accurate as markets evolve - continuous drift monitoring, automated retraining pipelines, performance reporting, and incident response. We stay engaged after delivery, because a model that is not maintained is a depreciating asset.

Why Vertical AI

Generic foundation models - GPT-4, Gemini, Claude - are excellent general-purpose tools. They are poor domain-specific tools. A fraud model needs to have seen UAE card transaction fraud patterns. A property valuation model needs to have seen DLD transaction history. A clinical risk model needs to have been validated on UAE patient population data.

Vertical AI models trained on your domain data consistently outperform generic models on your specific use cases by 15-30% on structured prediction tasks and 20-40% on domain-specific NLP tasks. This performance difference translates directly into business outcomes: lower fraud losses, better credit risk management, more accurate clinical decisions, more efficient retail operations.
